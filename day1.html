Ukrainian Adventure
Day One

This was the 1st stop on our journey. This was a village where one of my auts lived and where my grandparents lived. We were exhausted from our flight the day before, but we all were also super excited! The eight hour time difference tried to catch up with us, but we kept pushing on.

Village: Dobromyl

Most of the cities and villages have I heart signs. This one is I love Dobromyl.

Chickens

My grandma, my nieces great-grandma just had several chicks hatch. We got to play with them.

Country Side

We got to explore local country side. We forgot hats, so we improvised with local vegetation.

Local Animals

Got to meet a friendly calf along the way. We were able to pet it for a few minutes.

River: Vyrva

The local river, used to be a lot deeper, but started to dry out over the years.

My Grandparents

It was great to see my grandparents. My niece and newphew also got to hear few stories!
