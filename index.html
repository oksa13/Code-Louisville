Ukraine

A trip down memory lane!

Ukraine is the largest all-European nation. The population of this country is 41.5 million people, making it eight- most populous country in Europe. Due to rich soils and favorable climate, Ukraine has highly developed crop export. The main outputs are grain, potatoes, sugar beets and sunflower oil. The landscape includes everything from rollong hills, mountains to prairies and two seas. Let's take a look at my trip through this wonderful country!


More about Ukraine

Background

The trip to Ukraine began as an idea to show my niece and nephew a country where their grandparents were from and their mom was born. We visited the city of Lviv, Kyiv (capital of Ukraine), Carpathian mountains and country side. We walked many miles through museums and got to meet many relatives.

We actually had a 10 day trip, but with a day traveling each way, we only had eight solid days to explore one of the biggest countries in Europe. We had children events planned in each day. My niece was 12 and newphew was 8 years of age. They loved every moment of the trip and it was a great bonding opportunity for all of us!
